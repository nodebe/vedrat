How it works

Vedrat is an online plaform where business owners and organisers can leverage the social media pages of other users to advertise their products and services.

There are two plans which users can subscribe to:

Plan 1

User registers with N3000. In this plan you can only apply for one ad a day and get paid N280 daily and earn N7000.

Plan 2

User registers with N5000. In this plan you can only apply for two ads a day and get paid N560 daily and earn N14000.

Which payment provider do you accept?

We currently accept payments via Paystack.

Can I upgrade my subscription later?

Yes, you can upgrade your subscription, but your current subscription has to expire which happens 25 days after you subscribe and withdraw your earnings.

Can I post the ads on multiple social media platforms?

Yes, you can post the ads on multiple platforms, but it is most preferable to post on the facebook social media platform.

Referral System

Referrals in Plan 1

  • For every of your referral you earn N300.
  • For 10 of your referrals you get N3000.
  • For 20 of your referrals you get N7000.
  • For 50 of your referrals you get N20000.

If you have 20 referrals in plan 1:
N300 * 20 = N6000 + N3000(for 10 referrals) + N7000(for 20 referrals)
Total = N16000

Referrals in Plan 2

  • For every of your referral you earn N500.
  • For 10 of your referrals you get N5000.
  • For 20 of your referrals you get N12000.
  • For 50 of your referrals you get N32000.

If you have 20 referrals in plan 2:
N500 * 20 = N10000 + N5000(for 10 referrals) + N12000(for 20 referrals)
Total = N27000
